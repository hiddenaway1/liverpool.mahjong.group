What is Liverpool Riichi Mahjong

We are a casual Riichi Mahjong group that meets semi-regularly in and around Liverpool, UK.

We are a mix of new players, seasoned players, and everyone in between.

When and where are the meets?

Meet ups are not yet held regularly, but are instead arranged via the JanKenRon Discord server, full details below.

Join Us!

We are currently meeting semi-regularly at Genting Casino on Renshaw Street. We are looking at alternative locations that are easier to access but if you know of any alternative locations or would like to offer to host our meets in future, please get in touch!
