What is Liverpool Riichi Mahjong

We are a casual Riichi Mahjong group that meets semi-regularly in and around Liverpool, UK.

We are a mix of new players, seasoned players, and everyone in between.

When and where are the meets?

Meet ups are not yet held regularly, but are instead arranged via the JanKenRon Discord server, find out more on the Join Us page.
